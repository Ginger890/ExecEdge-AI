Career Capital Options Analysis
Know where you are going before you tell the market you are moving.
◆
Run this before Agent 0B. Most executives start networking before they know which version of themselves to present. This agent analyzes your Career Capital Summary against three realistic paths, scores each against your stated preferences, and produces a ranked recommendation with honest trade-offs. The path you choose here shapes your resume, your LinkedIn, your outreach — everything downstream. Do not skip it.
Bring This From Agent 0
►Paste the section labeled "CAREER CAPITAL SUMMARY — START HERE" from Agent 0 into the first field below.
Step 1 — Your Career Capital
Career Capital Summary Required
Paste the full Career Capital Summary output from Agent 0. The richer this input, the more precise the options analysis.
Your Most Recent Role & Organization Required
Title, organization, dates. Include any notable context — client type, contract size, team size, clearance level if applicable.
Your Complete Career Arc Required
Every significant role — delivery side, management side, consulting, government, corporate. Dates and context. The more complete, the more accurate the path scoring.
Your Highest-Value Accomplishments Required
The 4–6 things you are most proud of — with specifics. Numbers, scale, difficulty, outcome. Do not be modest.
